ABOUT THIS PROJECT

Today in New York, approximately 3,000 New Yorkers are seriously injured and more than 200 are killed each year in traffic crashes and part of that caused by bike safety related issues. To create a safer cycling environment, NYC DOT worked on these two projects in Manhattan in last three years and the following map provides an overall land use and zoning information around these two projects.

This project services as the web tool for planners and decision makers to eplore two of the current NYC Department of Transportation bike lane projects and its existing conditions. This tool can be used for future analysis of bike lane usage, bus ridership, and other activities that share the same public space with those two newly constructed bike lanes.
